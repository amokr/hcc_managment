FACILITIES

 

AMBULANCE

In case of any emergency, Ambulance facility is available

here for 24*7.



MEDICAL OFFICERS

Three Full Time Medical Officers are available for general

consultations and treatment during the day.



VISITING SPECIALISTS

Visiting Consultants are available for different specialties

in Allopathy, Ayurveda and Homeopathy.



DENTAL FACILITIES

A full-fledged Dental unit with the Dental X-ray facility is

operating in the Health Care Centre.Two Visiting

Dental Surgeons are available.



NURSING FACILITIES

Night casualty is operating with a Medical Officer and

staff nurse till mid night.
